PANDA

🐼 Chill Out with the Gentle Giant – The Panda!

Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca

Origin: Mountain ranges of central China

🎋 Preferred Habitat:

Cool, damp bamboo forests in high mountain areas.

🥢 Wild Abilities:

- Eats up to 12 hours a day, mostly bamboo!

- Extra thumb-like wrist bone to grip bamboo.

- Peaceful and shy – but strong and excellent climbers.

🐼 Fun Fact: Panda cubs are born smaller than a stick of butter!

Giant pandas are native to China and are known for their distinctive black and white fur. They primarily eat bamboo and are considered a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and low birth rates.

🐼 Fun Fact: Pandas have a special wrist bone that acts like a thumb, helping them grasp bamboo!
