ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY

🐒 Swing in for a Laugh with the Playful Monkey!

Scientific Name: (Varies by species – e.g., Macaca, Cebus, Mandrillus)

Origin: Africa, Asia, and Central/South America

🌴 Preferred Habitat:

Tropical rainforests, mountain forests, and even savannas.

🧠 Wild Abilities:

- Super smart and curious!

- Use tools and learn tricks.

- Agile climbers with tails that act like extra limbs (in some species)!

🐒 Fun Fact: Capuchin monkeys have been known to use stones to crack nuts!

Monkeys are a group of primates that are known for their intelligence, social behavior, and agility. They are found in various habitats, including tropical rainforests, savannas, and mountains. Monkeys are highly social animals and live in troops or groups. They communicate with each other using vocalizations, facial expressions, and body language.

Monkeys are omnivorous and have a varied diet that includes fruits, leaves, seeds, insects, and small animals. They are known for their dexterous hands and prehensile tails, which help them navigate through trees and grasp objects. Monkeys play an important role in their ecosystems by dispersing seeds and pollinating plants.
