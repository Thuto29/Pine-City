LION

🦁 Meet the Mighty Lion – King of the Savanna!

Scientific Name: Panthera leo

Origin: Africa (Sub-Saharan plains and grasslands)

🏞 Preferred Habitat:

Open woodlands, grasslands, and savannas where they can bask in the sun and rule their territory.

💪 Wild Abilities:

- Loudest roar in the animal kingdom – heard up to 8 km away!

- Social cats – they live in family groups called prides.

- Powerful hunters, working in teams to bring down large prey.

🦁 Fun Fact: Lions rest up to 20 hours a day!
